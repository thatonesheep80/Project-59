About

My favourite hobby is to doodle, or draw. Whenever I feel bored, I look at a image, draw its main parts, and doodle inside them. Some of my doodles have taken many hours to complete. I always feel happy when I listen to calm music and do this art. It has helped me to concentrate and complete a lot of my work quicker and better.
